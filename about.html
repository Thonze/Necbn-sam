About Organisation

NECABN supports empower Nigerians with the tools and resources they need to lead healthy, productive lives.

Our believe is that all Nigerians, no matter their life circumstances, deserve the opportunity to live a healthy and productive lives. They also can be a critical, often untapped resource for every society. Our strategic investments in people within communities unlock the potential for people to change their lives and to lead change in the world around them.

OUR CORE OBJECTIVES

Educate, empower and train Nigerians to participate actively in National Development.

Inculcate in Nigerians National values and right attitude for sustainable development, peacefull co-existence and tolerance for one another.

Undertake awareness programmes for Nigeria women so as to enable them identify fully in the affairs of the country and to attain self actualization and empowerment.

Generate ideas through seminars, symposia, workshops, debates and rallies at the grassroot and among the youths towards achieving better living standard and bring out full awareness on all governmental programmes.

Promote grassroot educational mobilization and to some extent provide aid to indigent students of educational institutions.
